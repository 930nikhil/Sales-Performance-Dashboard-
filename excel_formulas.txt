
EXCEL FORMULAS FOR SALES DASHBOARD
===================================

ðŸ“Š BASIC CALCULATIONS (Assuming data in columns A-O)
---------------------------------------------------

1. Total Sales
=SUM(L:L)

2. Total Profit
=SUM(O:O)

3. Total Orders
=COUNTA(A:A)-1

4. Average Order Value
=SUM(L:L)/COUNTA(A:A)

5. Profit Margin %
=SUM(O:O)/SUM(L:L)*100

6. Total Customers
=SUMPRODUCT(1/COUNTIF(E:E,E:E&""))

7. Average Discount
=AVERAGE(N:N)*100


ðŸ“… DATE-BASED FORMULAS
---------------------

8. Monthly Sales (with SUMIFS)
=SUMIFS($L:$L,$B:$B,">="&DATE(2024,1,1),$B:$B,"<"&DATE(2024,2,1))

9. Year-to-Date Sales
=SUMIFS($L:$L,$B:$B,">="&DATE(YEAR(TODAY()),1,1),$B:$B,"<="&TODAY())

10. Quarter Sales (Q1 Example)
=SUMIFS($L:$L,$B:$B,">="&DATE(2024,1,1),$B:$B,"<"&DATE(2024,4,1))


ðŸŽ¯ CONDITIONAL CALCULATIONS
--------------------------

11. Sales by Region (Example: East)
=SUMIF(H:H,"East",L:L)

12. Profit by Category (Example: Technology)
=SUMIF(I:I,"Technology",O:O)

13. Orders with Discount
=COUNTIF(N:N,">0")

14. High Value Orders (>$1000)
=COUNTIF(L:L,">1000")


ðŸ“ˆ ADVANCED FORMULAS
-------------------

15. Top 5 Products (Array Formula)
=INDEX($J:$J,MATCH(LARGE($O:$O,ROW(A1)),$O:$O,0))

16. Running Total
=SUM($L$2:L2)

17. Percentage of Total
=L2/SUM($L:$L)*100

18. Rank by Profit
=RANK(O2,$O:$O,0)

19. Moving Average (3 rows)
=AVERAGE(L2:L4)

20. Growth Rate (Month over Month)
=(L2-L3)/L3*100


ðŸ’¡ PIVOT TABLE FORMULAS
-----------------------

21. Distinct Count of Customers
In Pivot Table Field: Value Field Settings > Distinct Count

22. Profit Margin in Pivot
Calculated Field: =Profit/Sales

23. Average Order Value
Calculated Field: =Sales/COUNT(Order_ID)


ðŸŽ¨ CONDITIONAL FORMATTING FORMULAS
---------------------------------

24. Highlight Negative Profit
=O2<0

25. Highlight Top 10% Sales
=L2>=PERCENTILE($L:$L,0.9)

26. Color Scale by Discount
=N2>0.15

27. Flag Low Margin Orders
=O2/L2<0.15


ðŸ“Š DASHBOARD KPI FORMULAS
------------------------

28. Total Revenue Card
=TEXT(SUM(L:L),"$#,##0")

29. Profit Growth Indicator
=IF((SUM(L:L)-SUM(Previous_Month_Sales))/SUM(Previous_Month_Sales)>0,"â†‘","â†“")

30. Performance vs Target
=SUM(L:L)/Target_Value
